 # Naruto Dialogues: Japanese Originals with Translation

     Naruto Uzumaki (うずまきナルト)
    Japanese:
    「俺がなるってばよ！火影に！」
    Romaji:
    Ore ga naruttebayo! Hokage ni!
    English:
    "I'm gonna become Hokage, believe it!"

     Itachi Uchiha (うちはイタチ)
    Japanese:
    「許せサスケ、これで最後だ。」
    Romaji:
    Yuruse Sasuke, kore de saigo da.
    English:
    "Forgive me, Sasuke. This is the last time."

     Kakashi Hatake (はたけカカシ)
    Japanese:
    「仲間を大切にしないやつはクズだ。」
    Romaji:
    Nakama wo taisetsu ni shinai yatsu wa kuzu da.
    English:
    "Those who don’t care about their comrades are worse than scum."

     Pain / Nagato (ペイン / 長門)
    Japanese:
    「痛みを知らなければ、人は他人を理解できない。」
    Romaji:
    Itami wo shiranakereba, hito wa tanin wo rikai dekinai.
    English:
    "Those who do not understand pain can never truly understand others."
     Jiraiya (自来也)

    Japanese:
    「人は死ぬが、死に様は永遠に残る。」
    Romaji:
    Hito wa shinu ga, shinisama wa eien ni nokoru.
    English:
    "People die, but how they live and die remains in the hearts of others forever."
